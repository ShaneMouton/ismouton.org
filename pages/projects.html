MooBox


MooBox is an interface designed to bring retro games to your mobile devices. Since projects compiled with Emscripten had no interface for mobile devices, I set out to solve that with MooBox. Now a developer can compile their project with Emscripten incluce their wasm file from Emscriptem with MooBox and configure their button configuration and now they have a mobile friendly app!
